## Protocol kind of thing

Whiteboard size: 1280 x 720

### Messages
| message | effect |
|---------|--------|
| `p_(x)_(y)_(c)` | Draw at (x, y) with colour (c) |
| `l_(x1)_(y1)_(x2)_(y2)_(c)` | Draw a line from (x1, y1) to (x2, y2) with colour (c) |
| `c` | Clear the screen |

#### Colours
`0`: black

`1`: white
